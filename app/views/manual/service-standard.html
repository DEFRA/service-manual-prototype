Services must support Defra's sustainability and Net Zero goals.

Defra Standard

  • Hosting efficiency: You must demonstrate that you are resizing servers to match demand, for example by scaling down at night to reduce energy consumption.

Standards and assurance

Meeting the Service Standard at Defra

All public facing transaction services must meet the Government Service Standard (opens in new tab).

At Defra, we apply this standard to ensure our services work for users in rural environments, comply with environmental regulations, and integrate with our scientific data platforms.
