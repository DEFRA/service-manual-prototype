Greener service principles

The Greener Service Principles are cross-government guidance to help teams design, build and run lower-impact digital services.

Defra adopts these principles and expects service teams to consider them alongside the Service Standard when making technology and design decisions.

Detailed guidance on each principle will be added here. In the meantime, you can read the current version on GOV.UK.
