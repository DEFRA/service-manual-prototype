How to meet the standard

Assess risk and write statements

There are three broad steps services need to go through.

1. Risk evaluation

When: Before Alpha

Services should complete the Digital Sustainability Risk Evaluation. This points to the main risks a project will likely face.

2. Sustainability statement

When: From Alpha onwards

Each service should fill out and update a Digital Sustainability Statement. This allows the service to document actions taken against the 6 key objectives.

3. Service assessments

From 1 April 2026

The 15th Service Standard point will be mandated. Initially, there will be an ‘advisory’ RAG rating given.
